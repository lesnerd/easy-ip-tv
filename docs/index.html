Easy IPTV

Live TV, Movies & Shows

About

Easy IPTV is a multi-platform IPTV player for iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple TV. Add your M3U playlists, Xtream Codes, or Stalker Portal sources and start streaming live TV, movies, and shows.

Getting Started

  • Open the app and go to the Settings tab
  • Tap Add Playlist and choose your input method (M3U URL, Xtream Codes, or Stalker Portal)
  • Enter your playlist URL or credentials and tap Add
  • Your channels, movies, and shows will load automatically

Features

  • Live TV with instant channel switching
  • Movies and Shows (VOD) with cover art and details
  • Favorites for quick access
  • Language-based category sorting
  • Continue Watching and Recently Watched
  • Search across your entire library

Premium

Easy IPTV is free to use with ads. Upgrade to Premium ($11.90/year) for an ad-free experience, unlimited playlists, unlimited favorites, stream quality selection, subtitle support, and full watch history.
