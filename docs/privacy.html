Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Overview

Easy IPTV ("the App") is developed by Easy Company. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

All data stays on your device:

  • Playlist URLs and credentials — stored locally on your device only
  • Favorites and watch history — stored locally on your device only
  • App preferences (language, quality settings) — stored locally on your device only

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (iOS only, free tier) — displays banner and interstitial ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — processes in-app purchases. Apple handles all payment data. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Premium subscribers are not shown any ads, and no ad-related data is collected for Premium users.

IPTV Content

The App is a media player that connects to IPTV services you provide. We do not host, provide, or control any streaming content. You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to access the content from your IPTV provider.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties, except as described in the Third-Party Services section above.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@easyiptv.app.
